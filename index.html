About

Yoon Sang Cho has expertise in statistical and machine learning modeling in time series and sequential data. He is also interested in adversarial attacks and defense in AI modeling. He has been involved in numerous industrial projects (including smart manufacturing and healthcare areas) during his Ph.D. He served at the NYU Grossman School of Medicine and Samsung Medical Center as a postdoctoral fellow. He is currently a research professor at Korea University College of Medicine and a lecturer at Ewha Womans University. His research interests include trustworthy AI for industrial and healthcare applications.
